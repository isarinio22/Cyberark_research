Introducing Adaptive Identity Guardian

Adaptive Identity Guardian is a cloud-native, AI-powered Identity Governance and Administration (IGA) and Privileged Access Management (PAM) platform. We deliver enterprise-grade identity security and compliance with unparalleled simplicity, transparent pricing, and proactive automation.

Our focus shifts the paradigm from reactive threat detection to continuous identity posture management and automated risk remediation, specifically tailored for the unique needs of mid-market enterprises.
